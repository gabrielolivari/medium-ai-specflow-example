# Risk Matrix

Keep this file lightweight and actionable.

## Rules

- Maximum 5 risks per user story.
- Mitigation is mandatory for `Medium` and `High`.
- `Low` risks may be marked as monitor-only.

## Scale

- Probability: `Low` | `Medium` | `High`
- Impact: `Low` | `Medium` | `High`

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Priority | Mitigation | Status |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Example: Unauthorized access to admin route | Medium | High | High | Add server-side guard + 403 tests | Open |
